How did I get started?

Before programming, I worked as a physical therapist assistant (PTA). While working as a PTA in a long-term care facility, I knew that I had a knack for solving problems with the use of exercise prescription and therapy modalities. What I DIDN’T realize was how those problem- solving skills would later leak into other areas of my workplace. One day, while I was treating a total-knee replacement patient in the clinic, I ran out to the front entrance of the building to reset the safety alarm because a visitor had tried to exit the building without entering the proper code. A few minutes later, I saw another therapist go out and help someone with the same issue. By the end of the day, I had used my basic knowledge of Microsoft Word and my love for great customer service to post geriatric-friendly, easy-to-follow signs that explained, step-by-step, how to enter and exit the building safely, without tripping the alarm. Visitors were happy because they didn’t get embarrassed from tripping the alarm, my co-workers rejoiced because they didn’t have to leave their patients to reset the alarm, and my boss rejoiced because I helped improve her team’s productivity numbers! And now I’m a student at Lambda School and Free Code Camp, continuing my pursuit of learning to solve problems using technology!
